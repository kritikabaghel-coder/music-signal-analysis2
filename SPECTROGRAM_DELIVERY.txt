═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STFT PARAMETERS EXPLAINED
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

n_fft = 2048:
- FFT window size in samples
- Frequency resolution: 22050 Hz / 2048 ≈ 10.8 Hz
- Good balance for music analysis
- Creates 1025 frequency bins (n_fft/2 + 1)

hop_length = 512:
- Samples between consecutive frames
- Creates 75% window overlap (2048 - 512 = 1536 overlap)
- Time resolution: 512 / 22050 ≈ 23.2 ms per frame
- Standard choice for music

Trade-off:
- Larger n_fft: Better frequency resolution, worse time tracking
- Smaller n_fft: Better time tracking, worse frequency resolution
- Current choice (2048/512) balances both for music

GENRE VISUALIZATION CHARACTERISTICS

Rock/Metal:
- Strong vertical bands (sustained notes)
- High-energy across frequency range
- Clear percussion transients at bottom

Jazz:
- Complex overlapping patterns
- Wandering frequency traces (improvisation)
- Varied intensity (dynamic range)

Classical:
- Smooth sustained regions (orchestral strings)
- Clear harmonic series (horizontal bands)
- Wide frequency range utilization

Hip-Hop:
- Very strong bottom (prominent kick drum)
- Concentrated mid-range (vocals)
- Regular beat patterns

Pop:
- Compressed dynamic range (consistent brightness)
- Strong vocal presence (mid-range)
- Clear beat structure

Blues:
- Sustained bends (diagonal lines)
- Rich harmonics (multiple vertical bands)
- Warm mid-range

Country:
- Acoustic guitar presence (mid frequencies)
- Clear vocal definition
- Emphasis on harmonic content

Disco:
- Powerful bass line (bottom)
- Steady rhythm visible (beats)
- Consistent high-frequency shine

Reggae:
- Heavy bass emphasis
- Regular syncopated rhythms
- Steady, hypnotic patterns
